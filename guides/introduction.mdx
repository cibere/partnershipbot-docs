## What are guides?

Guides are like a short tutorial that show you how to do something that you might want to know to help have an easier time using the bot.

## Where can I suggest a guide?

If you join our [discord server](https://discord.gg/aCJtk2Y8rh), you can create a post in [#suggestions](https://canary.discord.com/channels/1029586455737159730/1130175180879183983)
